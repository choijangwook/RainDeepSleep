Sleep doesn’t always come when you need it. <br>
Some nights, the mind wanders, and the weight of<br>
the day lingers quietly in the dark. <br>

On nights like these, you don’t have to fight it.<br>
Just listen—to the gentle rhythm of rain,<br>
Let the sound wrap around you like a warm embrace,<br>
slowly easing every thought, guiding you into<br>
a deep, peaceful sleep. Breathe… slowly, gently.<br>

There is nowhere else you need to be. If this<br>
moment brings you even a little comfort,<br>
stay with us. You’ve done enough for today.<br>
Now… rest.<br>
