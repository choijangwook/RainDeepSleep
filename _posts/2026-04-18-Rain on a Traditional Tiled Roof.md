The rain falls softly onto the traditional tiled roof, gently touching each round, old tile,
and the smell of wet tiles and rainwater dripping from the eaves floats through the air like a warm, familiar hug.
The raindrops tap gently on the old, friendly tiled roof, making your eyes feel heavy and slow,
and you slowly drift off to sleep in the warm, cozy darkness of the traditional tiled house.<br>

젖은 기와와 처마에서 떨어지는 빗물 냄새가 따뜻하고 정겨운 포옹처럼 공기 속에 은은히 떠돕니다.

당신은 전통 기와집의 따뜻하고 아늑한 어둠 속에서 천천히 잠 속으로 빠져듭니다.
