The rain falls softly onto the lake, gently touching the wide, calm surface of the water,
and the cool, fresh smell of the lake floats through the air, wrapping around you like a soft, cozy blanket.
The raindrops tap gently on the wide, still lake, making little round circles on the water, making your eyes feel heavy,
and you slowly drift off to sleep in the quiet, soft darkness over the peaceful lake.
