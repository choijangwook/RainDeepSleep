The rain falls softly onto the mountain stream, making tiny little ripples on the water,
and the smell of cool, clean stream water floats through the air, wrapping around you like a soft blanket.
The raindrops tap gently on the bubbling stream, making your eyes feel heavy and slow,
and you slowly drift off to sleep in the quiet, soft darkness of the mountain stream.
