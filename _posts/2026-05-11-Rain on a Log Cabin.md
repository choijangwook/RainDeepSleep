The rain falls softly onto the log cabin, gently touching the big, rough wooden logs,
and the deep, warm smell of wet wood floats through the air, wrapping around you like a cozy, warm hug.
The raindrops tap gently on the old, sturdy log roof and walls, making your eyes feel heavy and slow,
and you slowly drift off to sleep in the warm, cozy darkness of the little log cabin.
