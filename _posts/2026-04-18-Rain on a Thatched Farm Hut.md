The rain falls softly onto the thatched farm hut, gently touching the old golden straw,
and the smell of wet straw and warm earth floats through the air like a friendly, cozy hug.
The raindrops tap gently on the thick, old thatched rooftop, making your eyes feel heavy and slow,
and you slowly drift off to sleep in the warm, cozy darkness of the little farm hut.
