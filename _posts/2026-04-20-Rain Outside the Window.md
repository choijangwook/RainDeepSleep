The rain falls softly outside the window, gently touching the cool glass all around,
and the fresh smell of rain seeps through the air, spreading a sleepy, cozy feeling all over you.
The raindrops tap and slide down the window glass, making your eyes feel heavy and slow,
and you slowly drift off to sleep in the quiet, soft darkness filled with the sound of rain outside.
