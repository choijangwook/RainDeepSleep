The rain seeps quietly through the leaves deep into the forest,
and the moss-scented air fills your lungs, softly melting away every tension in your body.
The sound of raindrops tapping on layers of fallen leaves weighs gently on your eyelids,
as your mind slowly sinks into the deep and silent darkness of the forest.<br>

당신의 마음은 숲의 깊고 고요한 어둠 속으로 천천히 가라앉습니다.
