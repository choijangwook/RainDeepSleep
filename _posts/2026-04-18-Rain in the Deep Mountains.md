The rain falls softly in the deep mountains, quiet as a whisper,
and the cool, clean mountain air wraps around you like a cozy blanket.
The raindrops tap gently on the rocks and grass, making your eyes feel heavy,
and you slowly drift off to sleep in the peaceful, dark mountains.<br>

당신은 고요하고 어두운 산속에서 천천히 잠 속으로 빠져듭니다.
